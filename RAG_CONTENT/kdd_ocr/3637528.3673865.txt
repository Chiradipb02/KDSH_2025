ABSTRACT
The Finance Day at KDD 2024 will take place on August 26th in
Barcelona, Spain. Following the success of the inaugural event last
year, the second edition highlights the significant role of AI in
transforming the financial industry. This special day serves as a
forum for discussion of innovations at the intersection of AI and
finance. An exciting lineup of 12 influential speakers from nine dif-
ferent countries will be featured, representing a mix of government
organizations, leading banks, innovative hedge funds, and top aca-
demic institutions. These experts will delve into a range of topics,
from cutting-edge FinTech innovations to ethical considerations
in machine learning, providing a comprehensive overview of the
finance and AI. The distinguished speakers include Avanidhar Sub-
rahmanyam from UCLA, Henrike Mueller from the Financial Con-
duct Authority, Claudia Perlich from Two Sigma, Eyke Hüllermeier
from Ludwig-Maximilians-Universität München, Senthil Kumar
from Capital One, Stefan Zohren from the University of Oxford,
Dumitru Roman from SINTEF ICT, Kubilay Atasu from TU Delft,
Xiao-Ming Wu from Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Yongjae
Lee from UNIST, Jundong Li from the University of Virginia, and
Milos Blagojevic from BlackRock.

1 INTRODUCTION
It is with immense pleasure that we welcome you to the Finance
Day at KDD 2024 on August 26, 2024, in Barcelona, Spain. Follow-
ing the success of our inaugural event last year, we are hosting the
second edition of Finance Day at the ACM Knowledge Discovery
and Data Mining (KDD) conference. This special day highlights the
pivotal role that AI, and in particular machine learning and data
mining, play in transforming the financial industry. In times where
financial decisions are increasingly data-driven, this event serves as
a forum to present the latest innovations from research groups in
both academia and industry that will help foster public-private col-
laborations. This year’s Finance Day features 12 influential speakers
giving talks that capture the forefront of financial technology and
